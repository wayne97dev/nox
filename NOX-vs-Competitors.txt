NOX vs. THE COMPETITION — STEALTH ADDRESSES vs. MIXERS
=======================================================

Mixers (Tornado Cash being the archetype) buy privacy by pooling
everyone's funds together: you deposit, wait, and withdraw to a clean
address. It works — but it carries three problems that are fatal for a
project meant to last. NOX takes a different route: stealth addresses
(the ERC-5564 standard), privacy on the receiver's side, without ever
touching anyone else's money.

WHY WE CHOSE STEALTH ADDRESSES
------------------------------

1) NO COMMINGLING = FAR LOWER LEGAL SURFACE
   A mixer pools strangers' money together — and it is exactly that
   "mixing of third-party funds" that makes it look, to regulators, like
   an unlicensed money transmitter. The precedents are heavy: OFAC
   sanctioned Tornado Cash (Aug 2022); in the Netherlands, developer
   Alexey Pertsev was CONVICTED of money laundering (May 2024); in the
   US, criminal charges were brought against the founders. The decisive
   detail: even after the protocol sanctions were partly overturned in
   court and the entity was delisted (2025), the criminal cases against
   the developers continued. So the risk is not a blocklist — it is
   PERSONAL and ARCHITECTURAL. Stealth addresses pool nothing: they are
   just a way to compute a receiving address. A neutral, standardized
   primitive.

2) PURELY NON-CUSTODIAL, ZERO TAINT
   With a mixer you hand funds to a shared pool contract; on withdrawal,
   exchanges and chain-analysis can flag those funds as "coming from a
   mixer" -> frozen accounts, rejected deposits. With NOX, funds go
   DIRECTLY to your stealth address: no pool to sanction, no one else's
   "dirty" coins attached to yours.

3) STANDARD + INCENTIVE
   Stealth addresses are ERC-5564 / ERC-6538: an Ethereum standard,
   interoperable, that wallets are beginning to support. And NOX adds
   what mixers lack: it REWARDS usage with mining (1,000 NOX per stealth
   transaction, halving every 250k tx). You incentivize a legally
   neutral technology — not a high-risk service.


WHAT WE DON'T PROMISE (no smoke and mirrors)
--------------------------------------------

Stealth addresses hide the RECIPIENT, not everything: the SENDER and the
AMOUNT stay public on-chain. A mixer with a large anonymity set hides
more on that front. For users who want total privacy — sender AND amount
— NOX integrates an opt-in zk shielded pool (Railgun), designed to stay
compatible with compliance mechanisms. The user picks the level:

    public  ->  stealth (recipient hidden)  ->  Railgun (everything hidden)
